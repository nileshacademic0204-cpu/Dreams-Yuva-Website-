Our story

A youth-led movement creating sustainable environmental and social impact since 2016.

Dreams Yuva Social Foundation began with a simple idea on the eve of 2016. A group of young professionals from Uruli Kanchan decided to welcome the New Year through meaningful community service and social responsibility.

What started as a weekly village cleanliness drive gradually evolved into a powerful grassroots movement focused on environmental conservation, blood donation, disaster relief, youth leadership and community participation.

🌱

Our Vision

Creating environmentally conscious and socially responsible communities.

To develop sustainable and replicable community models that create environmental awareness and inspire positive social transformation.

🎯

Our Mission

Empowering people through participation, collaboration and action.

To build strong partnerships with communities and local institutions, implement sustainable solutions and promote environmental conservation.

Our Work Areas

Green Cover Restoration

Creating greener communities through large-scale plantation, ecological restoration, and long-term environmental conservation initiatives.

Blood Donation & Emergency Support

Organizing blood donation camps and supporting emergency blood requirements to save lives and strengthen healthcare support systems.

Plastic Bank & Waste Management

Promoting responsible plastic waste management through collection drives, recycling awareness, and active community participation.

Gram Swachhata Abhiyan

Driving village cleanliness and environmental awareness through regular volunteer-led campaigns and citizen engagement activities.

Disaster Relief & Community Support

Providing emergency support, relief materials, and rehabilitation assistance during natural disasters and humanitarian crises.
