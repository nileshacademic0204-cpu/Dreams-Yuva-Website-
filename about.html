Our Story

A youth-led movement creating sustainable environmental and social impact since 2016.

Dreams Yuva Social Foundation was established in 2016 by a group of passionate young professionals from Uruli Kanchan who believed that meaningful change begins with community participation and collective action.

What started as a village cleanliness initiative has grown into a grassroots movement working in environmental conservation, blood donation, disaster relief, waste management, and youth leadership development across multiple communities.

Today, Dreams Yuva Social Foundation continues to inspire volunteers, institutions, and local communities to work together for a greener, healthier, and more responsible society.

Our Journey

From a village cleanliness initiative to a community-driven movement, our journey reflects the power of collective action, environmental stewardship, and youth leadership.
